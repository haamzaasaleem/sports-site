Our Story

BORN FROM FAITH.
BUILT FOR YOUTH.

Rising Empowerment Sports Ministries was founded by Ferman Perez in Winton, CA with a simple but powerful vision — to use the power of sport to transform the lives of young people in the community.

What started as a desire to give kids a safe place to compete has grown into a full nonprofit ministry serving youth through multiple programs, each rooted in faith and designed to develop total athletes for life.

We believe every child is created with purpose. Our role is to walk alongside them, help them discover that purpose, and equip them with the tools — athletic, spiritual, and personal — to fulfill it.

What We Stand For

OUR CORE VALUES

Every program, every practice, every interaction is guided by these principles that define who we are and how we serve.

✞
Faith First

Everything we do is rooted in the Gospel. We believe sport is a platform for ministry, and every athlete is a child of God with eternal purpose.

🏆
Excellence

We pursue excellence in everything — on the field and off. We challenge our youth to give their best and honor God through the way they compete.

🤝
Community

We build a family where every child belongs. Our doors are open to all — regardless of background, ability level, or circumstance.

🌟
Empowerment

We don't just coach athletes — we raise leaders. We equip young people with the skills and confidence to succeed in every area of life.

💪
Resilience

We teach our youth to rise above setbacks. Life will challenge them — we prepare them to face those challenges with strength and faith.

❤️
Redemption

We believe in second chances. No matter where a young person comes from, there is always a path forward — and we are committed to walking it with them.
