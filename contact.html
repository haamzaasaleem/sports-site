Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We strive to keep our programs accessible to all youth regardless of financial situation. Please contact us directly to discuss program fees and scholarship opportunities for families in need.
Our programs are designed to serve youth of various age groups. Contact us for specific age requirements for each program — we work hard to ensure every child has a place in our community.
Our programs are open to all youth. While our ministry is faith-based and centered on Christian values, every child is welcome regardless of their religious background. We simply ask for respect for our faith-centered environment.
You can donate through our Get Involved page or by contacting us directly via email or phone. We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, so all donations are tax deductible.
We welcome volunteers with a variety of skills — coaches, mentors, event helpers, media/photography, and more. Fill out our volunteer inquiry form on the Get Involved page or reach out to us directly and we'll find the right fit for you.
