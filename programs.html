OUR PROGRAMS

From competitive sports teams to at-risk youth programs and chess clubs — every program is built on faith, purpose, and the belief that every child deserves a chance to rise.

Sports Team

THE WARRIORS

Our flagship sports team where young athletes compete, grow, and are rooted in faith. The Warriors are more than a team — they are a family that plays with purpose on and off the field.

Every practice, every game, every interaction is an opportunity to build character, develop discipline, and grow closer to God.

  • Competitive team sports in multiple disciplines
  • Faith-based coaching and mentorship
  • Character development through competition
  • Open to youth of all skill levels
  • Community events and outreach

At-Risk Youth

EMPOWERMENT REDEMPTION LEAGUE

The ERL is specifically designed for at-risk youth — young people who need more than a game to play. They need a safe space, a community, and a path forward. That's exactly what we provide.

Through structured sports programming, mentorship, and faith-based support, the ERL walks alongside young people who are facing real challenges and gives them real hope.

  • Safe, structured environment for at-risk youth
  • One-on-one and group mentorship
  • Sports-based rehabilitation and confidence building
  • Faith-centered support and counseling
  • Pathways to other RESM programs
❤️

Redemption is Real

No matter where a young person has been, we believe their best days are ahead. The ERL exists to prove that — one life at a time.

Mental Skills

WARRIORS CHESS CLUB

Champions think before they act. The Warriors Chess Club was created to develop the mental side of our athletes — building strategic thinking, patience, focus, and discipline.

We believe a total athlete is not just physically fit — they are mentally sharp. Chess teaches life lessons that carry far beyond the board.

  • Weekly chess instruction for all skill levels
  • Strategic thinking and problem-solving development
  • Patience, focus, and emotional discipline
  • Faith-based leadership lessons through the game
  • Tournaments and competitive play
